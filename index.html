Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 09, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application Scripter, developed and maintained by Dartisan Inc.

Scripter is a simple, content-focused quotes app designed to share original writing and inspirational thoughts. We respect your privacy and want to be transparent about how your data is handled. The short answer: we don’t collect or store any personal information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require or support user account creation.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifiers.
  • We do not access or store any location data.
  • We do not access contacts, messages, camera, or photos.
  • We do not serve or track push notifications.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools in the app.

2. Data Storage and Transmission

All quotes are embedded in the app or fetched from a public, static source. No user data is sent to or received from any server owned or controlled by Dartisan Inc.

3. Third-Party Services

This app does not rely on third-party services that process user data. If this ever changes in a future update, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly and users will be notified.

4. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13, but since we do not collect any data, we are not collecting data from children either. If you believe a child has submitted personal information by mistake, please contact us at dartisan.inc@gmail.com.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. If a major change occurs, the update will be posted clearly within the app or on the app listing page. The date at the top of this policy reflects the last revision.
